Early adopter program

As a participant in our Early Adopter Program (EAP), you will play a pivotal role in the validation of our Mercury digital servo motors. In addition to receiving a significant discount on the normal purchase price, you will have the opportunity to provide suggestions on future product features and have early access to products on our roadmap.

How does the early adopter program work?

  • You receive a 20% discount on Mercury servos purchased for your EAP project
  • You report any issues directly through the EAP support process using eapsupport@robotarticulation.com
  • You provide periodic updates on your research and progress.
  • You get direct support from our technical team.
  • You provide quotes and case study material for our marketing purposes.

Eligibility requirements

  • Be enthusiastic and offer regular feedback
  • Apply to the program and be approved to participate
  • Complete the EAP application form.
  • Download, sign, and return the EAP agreement to eapsupport@robotarticulation.com
