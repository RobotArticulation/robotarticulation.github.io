Our goal at Robot Articulation?

Let's face it, building robots is a hard and difficult business.
At Robot Articulation, we want to help you:

Our Mercury digital servos have the precision and high torque capabilities required for many demanding robotics applications.
Our free cross-platform device driver and configuration application allow for the easy configuration and integration of Mercury digital servos into your system.
For easy integration with ROS (Robot Operating System), we will soon publish our Mercury ROS package on ROS.org.
